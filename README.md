📌 About
EVChargeMetrics is a Power BI analytics dashboard built on a cleaned EV charging station dataset, covering station-level performance, revenue analysis, energy dispensed, and operator-level breakdowns.
💡 Built as part of an AI-Driven Data Analytics Program — covering EV infrastructure across cities, operators, connector types, and utilization metrics.
What this dashboard covers:
	•	✅ EV charging station revenue and energy dispensed tracking
	•	✅ Station-level performance by operator, connector type, and region
	•	✅ Geographic revenue distribution across cities
	•	✅ Installation trends over time
	•	✅ Utilization analysis and revenue per kWh insights
	•	✅ Decomposition tree drill-downs for root cause analysis

🗂️ Column Reference
Ev charging Cleaned — Main Table



|# |Column                |Data Type|Description                                   |
|--|----------------------|---------|----------------------------------------------|
|1 |`Station ID`          |*Text*   |Unique identifier for each EV charging station|
|2 |`City`                |*Text*   |City where the station is located             |
|3 |`Opertors`            |*Text*   |Operator/company managing the station         |
|4 |`Operator Sector`     |*Text*   |Sector classification of the operator         |
|5 |`Connectors`          |*Text*   |Connector type at the station                 |
|6 |`Connectors Region`   |*Text*   |Regional grouping of connector types          |
|7 |`Speed/Connector`     |*Text*   |Charging speed — e.g., **Fast** / **Slow**    |
|8 |`Status`              |*Text*   |Station status — **Active** / **Inactive**    |
|9 |`Utilization`         |*Decimal*|Usage rate / utilization bucket               |
|10|`Installation Date`   |*Date*   |Date the station was installed                |
|11|`Revenue`             |*Decimal*|Revenue generated by the station              |
|12|`Revenue Per kWh`     |*Decimal*|Revenue earned per kilowatt-hour              |
|13|`Energy Dispensed kWh`|*Decimal*|Total energy dispensed in kWh                 |

Calculated Measures (DAX)



|#|Measure                  |Description                            |
|-|-------------------------|---------------------------------------|
|1|`Total Revenue`          |Sum of all station revenue             |
|2|`Total energy Dispensed` |Total kWh dispensed across all stations|
|3|`Average Revenue per kWh`|Avg revenue earned per unit energy     |
